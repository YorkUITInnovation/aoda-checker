Results Overview

Understanding Violations

Each violation includes: Error/Warning/Alert severity, WCAG criterion, description, affected element, and remediation guidance.

Severity Levels

Error: Critical issues that must be fixed
Warning: Important issues to address
Alert: Minor issues or best practice recommendations

Exporting Results

Download results as Word (DOCX) or Excel (XLSX) format. Share with your team for remediation tracking.

Printing Reports

Use the Print Results button to create printer-friendly reports for documentation.

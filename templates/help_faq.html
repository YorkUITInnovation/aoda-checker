Getting Started

Q: How do I start my first scan?
A: Enter a URL on the home page and click 'Start Accessibility Scan'.

Q: What does AODA mode mean?
A: AODA mode scans for WCAG 2.0 Level AA compliance, required by Ontario law.

Scanning Issues

Q: My scan is taking too long
A: Large sites take longer. Check estimated time or reduce Max Pages.

Q: Some pages aren't scanned
A: Pages might require authentication or be outside Max Depth setting.

Understanding Results

Q: What's the difference between Error/Warning/Alert?
A: Errors are critical, Warnings are important, Alerts are minor or best practices.

Q: How do I fix violations?
A: Each violation includes a help URL with detailed remediation guidance.

Technical Issues

Q: Can I scan password-protected sites?
A: Currently no, only publicly accessible pages can be scanned.

Q: How many pages can I scan?
A: Up to 10,000 pages per scan. Large scans use checkpointing for reliability.

Need More Help?

If you can't find an answer here, contact your system administrator or accessibility team lead for assistance.
